Welcome to RocketPaddle!

Use the mouse to move the paddle. Press and hold the Left Mouse Button to use the rockets.

While in the game's menu, press
L to see the leaderboards, or
A to see the achievement list.

Every block destroyed with the ball gives you 5 points, whereas every block destroyed with the paddle gives you 10 points.

If you want to get high scores, you have to use the paddle as much as possible - but be careful not to let the ball fall in.
